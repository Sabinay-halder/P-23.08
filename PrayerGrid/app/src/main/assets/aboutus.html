The Prayer Grid is a ministry of The Prayer People, a 501(c)(3) non-profit organization that specializes in outreach and evangelism. Our mission is to share the Gospel while creating a community of praying believers. The Prayer Grid was specially designed to build relationships within the church through the power of prayer!

Media Outreach

Our Media Outreach Division specializes in sharing the Gospel through the modern technology we have at our fingertips!

Discipleship Groups

Our Discipleship Groups meet around the world to share the Gospel while building relationships and Christian disciples!

Orphan Ministry

Every quarter, we send a shipment of supplies to orphan children who are in need of clothes, hygiene items, and more!
